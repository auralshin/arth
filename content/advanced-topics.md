### Advanced Topics

This module collects higher-level research topics that often require cross-disciplinary tooling: tokenomics, governance, systemic risk, and dynamic hedging.

- Tokenomics modeling: inflation schedules, supply sinks, staking ratios, and peg designs.
- Governance game theory: voting equilibria, bribery, and incentive design for protocol upgrade mechanisms.
- Risk management: collateral correlation matrices, CVaR, and stress-testing whole-protocol exposures.
- Yield aggregation: normalizing APYs across compounding conventions and multiple asset types.
- MEV arbitrage simulation: modeling searcher profitability distribution, and the impact of mitigation on user welfare.
- Dynamic hedging: delta-hedging with on-chain perps and options replication strategies.
